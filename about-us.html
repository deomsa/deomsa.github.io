Our purpose

The purpose of my shop is to be able to sell high end tea products. The quality of our products are above average of normal brands but for a reasonable price. We love tea so much that we want ever person to be able to afford tea and enjoy tea that actually taste good and without adding fake stuff. Please suport my business!

How did we start?

I have always enjoyed drinking tea. I have been to different tea house. For example, I have been to one in Topsaiil Beach, NC, Winchester, VA, and soon will be going to one in Canada this summer. I want to create a business to sell tea for a reasonable price that taste good. So a couple months ago I started Spillin' the Tea. I hope you all enjoy this company and the tea. Thank you for visiting this website. Drink tea!!!
